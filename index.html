Biography

I'm a student trader focused on swing setups in Indian large- and mid-cap equities, building the discipline of a professional desk: every idea gets a written thesis, a defined stop, and staged targets before entry — and every outcome, good or bad, gets logged publicly. I'm currently preparing for the NISM certifications and building out a Python-based screener to support idea generation. My goal is an internship in equity research or trading where that process-first approach adds value from day one.
